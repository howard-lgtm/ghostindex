## Day 6-7: First Interviews

### Goal: Conduct 2-3 interviews

**Pre-Interview Checklist:**
- [ ] Review their LinkedIn profile
- [ ] Note their company, industry, role
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 personalized questions
- [ ] Test Zoom/Meet link
- [ ] Have DISCOVERY_QUESTIONS.md open

**Interview Structure (20 minutes):**

**Minutes 0-2: Intro & Context**
- Thank them for their time
- Brief overview: "Building transparency tool for hiring"
- Explain: "No right answers, just want to learn from your experience"
- Ask permission to take notes

**Minutes 2-15: Discovery Questions**

*Start with pain points:*
1. "Walk me through your typical week - how much time goes to candidate follow-up?"
2. "What's the most frustrating part of your hiring workflow right now?"
3. "Have you ever lost a great candidate because your process was too slow?"

*Explore ghosting (both directions):*
4. "How often do candidates ghost you after interviews?"
5. "When you can't respond to every applicant, how do you handle that?"
6. "Do you think companies in your industry have a reputation problem with candidate experience?"

*Test value proposition:*
7. "If there was a public score showing how responsive companies are to applicants, would that concern you or excite you?"
8. "Would you pay for a tool that helped you improve your company's candidate response metrics?"
9. "How would you use data showing your company's hiring responsiveness compared to competitors?"

*Validate features:*
10. "If you could see which of your job postings were being flagged as 'ghost jobs' by applicants, would you act on that?"
11. "Would a 'verified responsive employer' badge be valuable for your job postings?"

**Minutes 15-18: Open Discussion**
- "What am I not asking that I should be?"
- "What tools do you currently use for candidate experience?"
- "If you could wave a magic wand, what would you fix about hiring?"

**Minutes 18-20: Wrap-Up**
- Thank them again
- Ask: "Would you be open to a follow-up once we build something?"
- Ask: "Anyone else I should talk to?" (referrals)
- Offer to share anonymized insights from other interviews

**Post-Interview:**
- [ ] Send thank you message within 24 hours
- [ ] Document key insights in notes template
- [ ] Update tracking spreadsheet
- [ ] Add any feature requests to backlog

## Common Objections & Responses

### "I don't have time"
**Response:** "Totally understand - would 15 minutes work instead? Or I can send you the questions via email if that's easier."

### "What's in it for me?"
**Response:** "I'll share anonymized insights from other recruiters - you'll see how your challenges compare to the industry. Plus, if we build this, you'll get early access."

### "This sounds like it could hurt companies"
**Response:** "Actually, the goal is to help companies improve. Think of it like Glassdoor - the best companies embrace transparency because it helps them stand out."

### "We already have tools for this"
**Response:** "That's great! I'd love to hear what's working and what's not. Your experience with existing tools is exactly what I need to understand."
