# GhostIndex Discovery Questions

**Purpose:** Validate product-market fit with Pro Recruiters and Enterprise customers

---

## For Pro Recruiters

### Understanding Their Pain Points

- How much time do you spend per week following up with candidates who never respond?
- What's the biggest frustration in your current hiring workflow?
- Have you ever lost a great candidate because your process was too slow? What happened?
- How do you currently track candidate communication and response times?
- What tools do you use to manage your hiring pipeline? What's missing?

### On Ghosting (Both Sides)

- How often do candidates ghost you after interviews? At what stage does it typically happen?
- When you can't respond to every applicant, how do you handle that communication gap?
- Do you think companies in your industry have a reputation problem with candidate experience?
- Have you seen negative reviews on Glassdoor or LinkedIn about hiring practices? How did the company respond?

### On GhostIndex Concept

- If there was a public score showing how responsive companies are to applicants, would that concern you or excite you?
- Would you pay for a tool that helped you improve your company's candidate response metrics?
- How would you use data showing your company's hiring responsiveness compared to competitors?
- Would a "verified responsive employer" badge be valuable for your job postings?

### Product Validation

- If you could see which of your job postings were being flagged as "ghost jobs" by applicants, would you act on that?
- What would make you recommend a hiring tool to other recruiters?
- How do you currently measure candidate experience? What metrics matter most?
- Would you be interested in benchmarking your response times against industry standards?

---

## For Enterprise / HR Leaders

### Strategic Concerns

- How important is employer brand to your talent acquisition strategy?
- Have you lost candidates to competitors because of perceived hiring process issues?
- How do you currently measure and report on candidate experience to leadership?
- What's your biggest challenge in scaling hiring while maintaining quality candidate experience?

### Reputation & Accountability

- If a third-party platform rated your company's hiring responsiveness, how would leadership react?
- Do you monitor what candidates say about your hiring process on social media or review sites?
- Have you ever had to do damage control after negative candidate experience stories went viral?
- How much budget do you allocate to employer branding vs. actual hiring process improvements?

### On GhostIndex Value Proposition

- Would you pay for a dashboard showing real-time candidate experience metrics across your organization?
- If improving your "Ghost Index Score" could increase applicant quality, what would that be worth?
- Would you want to be notified when your company's hiring responsiveness drops below industry benchmarks?
- How valuable would it be to show candidates a "verified responsive employer" certification?

### Integration & Adoption

- What would it take for you to integrate a new candidate experience tool into your ATS workflow?
- Who in your organization would own "candidate experience" as a metric?
- What existing tools would GhostIndex need to integrate with to be useful?
- How do you currently handle the handoff between recruiting and hiring managers on communication?

### Competitive Intelligence

- Would you pay for anonymized data on how your competitors handle candidate communication?
- How useful would industry benchmarks be for justifying investment in hiring process improvements?
- If you could see which companies in your industry are losing candidates due to slow responses, would that inform your strategy?

---

## For Both Audiences

### Pricing & Willingness to Pay

- What's your current budget for recruiting tools and candidate experience solutions?
- Would this be a per-seat, per-job-posting, or flat monthly fee that works best for you?
- At what price point would you consider this a "no-brainer" investment?
- What ROI would you need to see to justify the cost to leadership?

### Competition & Alternatives

- What do you use today to solve this problem (even partially)?
- What would make you switch from your current solution?
- Have you evaluated any other candidate experience tools? What was missing?

### Final Validation

- On a scale of 1-10, how painful is the problem we're describing?
- If this product existed today, would you sign up for a pilot?
- Who else should we be talking to about this?
- What would make you tell other recruiters/HR leaders about GhostIndex?

---

## Key Insights to Capture

After each interview, note:

1. **Pain Level (1-10):** How much does this problem actually hurt them?
2. **Current Solution:** What are they using today?
3. **Budget Authority:** Can they make purchasing decisions?
4. **Use Case:** B2B (improve their own score) or data consumer?
5. **Referrals:** Did they offer to connect us with others?
6. **Quote:** One memorable thing they said
