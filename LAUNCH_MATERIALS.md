# GhostIndex: A Transparency Platform for Job Seekers

**TL;DR:** I built a platform that tracks which companies ghost job applicants. Search companies, see their "Ghost Index Score," and share your experience. Live now at https://getghostindex.com

## The Problem

If you've ever applied for a job, you know the frustration:
- You spend hours crafting the perfect application
- You never hear back
- Not even a rejection email
- Complete radio silence

This is "ghosting," and it's become normalized in hiring. But it doesn't have to be this way.

## The Solution

GhostIndex is a transparency platform that:

1. **Tracks ghosting rates** - We calculate a "Ghost Index Score" (0-100) for each company based on verified reports
2. **Verified reports** - Users forward their job application confirmation emails to verify their experience
3. **Detailed company profiles** - See all reports, statistics, and trends for any company
4. **Auto-ghosting detection** - If no response after 30 days, we automatically mark it as ghosted

## How It Works

1. **Search** - Look up any company (90+ indexed so far)
2. **View Score** - See their Ghost Index Score and risk level
3. **Read Reports** - See what other applicants experienced
4. **Submit Your Own** - Share your experience to help others

## The Data

- **91 companies** indexed (FAANG, Big Tech, startups)
- **35 verified reports** (growing daily)
- **Ghost Index Scores** calculated in real-time
- **Email verification** ensures data quality

## Why I Built This

As a job seeker, I was tired of the information asymmetry. Companies have all the data about candidates, but candidates have no data about companies' hiring practices.

GhostIndex levels the playing field. If a company consistently ghosts applicants, that should be public information.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js 16, React, TailwindCSS
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth)
- **Email:** Mailgun for verification
- **Hosting:** Vercel
- **Security:** Rate limiting, input sanitization, RLS

## What's Next

This is a beta launch. I'm looking for:
- **Feedback** on the UX and features
- **Reports** from real job seekers
- **Company suggestions** to add to the database
- **Ideas** for improving the platform
